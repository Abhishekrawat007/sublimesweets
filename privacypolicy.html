Data Usage

How We Use Your Information

Clear purposes for every piece of data we collect from you.

Purpose-Driven Collection

We collect only the data necessary to provide our services. Your information is used to process orders, improve your experience, communicate important updates, ensure platform security, comply with legal requirements, and provide customer support. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

Third-Party Sharing

We share limited information only with trusted partners essential to our services: payment processors (Razorpay), delivery partners, cloud storage providers (Firebase), analytics tools (Google Analytics), and email service providers. All partners are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

Cookies & Tracking

Understanding Cookies

How we use cookies to improve your browsing experience.

Cookie Policy

We use cookies to remember your preferences, keep you logged in, analyze site usage, and deliver personalized content. You can control cookie settings through your browser. Essential cookies are required for the site to function, while analytics and marketing cookies require your consent.

Data Retention

How Long We Keep Your Data

Clear timelines for data storage and deletion.

Retention Periods

Active account data is retained as long as your account exists. After account deletion, personal data is removed within 30 days, except for legally required records (tax, orders) kept for 7 years. Anonymous analytics data may be retained indefinitely. You can request immediate deletion by contacting our privacy team.

Children's Privacy

Protecting Minors

Our commitment to safeguarding children's information.

Age Restrictions

Our services are intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will delete it promptly.
