How It Works

Simple Refund Process

We've made returns and refunds incredibly simple. Follow these easy steps to get your refund processed quickly.

Step 01

Contact Support

Reach out to our customer support team via phone, email, or WhatsApp within the eligible return window. Provide your order number and describe the issue.

Step 02

Share Details & Photos

Send clear photos of the product showing the issue. Our team will review your request and verify the problem to ensure quick resolution.

Step 03

Approval & Options

Once approved, you'll receive options for a full refund, partial refund, or product replacement based on your preference and product availability.

Step 04

Receive Your Refund

Your refund will be processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method. You'll receive confirmation via email and SMS.

Detailed Policies

What You Need to Know

Clear guidelines for different types of products and situations.

Order Cancellation

Cancel your order before it's processed or shipped for a full refund.

Free Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled free of charge before they enter the preparation or shipping stage.

  • Cancel within 10 minutes of placing order - 100% refund
  • Cancel before preparation starts - Full refund
  • Cancel after preparation - May incur charges
  • Cancel after dispatch - Return process required

Fresh Items

Special guidelines for perishable and fresh food items requiring immediate attention.

Time-Sensitive

Report any issues within 2 hours of delivery for fresh items. Quality and safety are our top priorities.

  • 2-hour window to report quality issues
  • Full refund for spoiled or stale items
  • Replacement available subject to stock
  • Photos required for verification

Packaged Items

Return policy for sealed and packaged products with longer shelf life.

24-Hour Window

Packaged items must be unopened and in original condition for returns within 24 hours of delivery.

  • 24-hour return window from delivery
  • Items must be unopened and sealed
  • Full refund for damaged packaging
  • Defective items replaced immediately

Custom Orders

Special terms for personalized and custom-made products.

Made To Order

Custom orders are prepared specifically for you and have different cancellation terms.

  • Cancel 48 hours before delivery - 50% refund
  • Cancel 24 hours before - 25% refund
  • Same-day cancellation - No refund
  • Quality issues - Full refund/replacement

Bulk Orders

Guidelines for large quantity orders and corporate purchases.

Corporate Terms

Bulk orders (10+ items) may have custom terms. Contact our corporate team for specific policies.

  • Custom terms for orders above ₹10,000
  • Advance notice required for cancellation
  • Partial refunds based on preparation stage
  • Dedicated support for bulk orders

Non-Refundable

Items and situations where refunds cannot be provided.

Please Note

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about refunds and cancellations.

How long does it take to receive my refund?

Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days from the date of approval. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your bank or payment provider. For UPI and wallet payments, refunds are usually instant to 24 hours. For credit/debit cards, it may take 5-7 business days. You will receive confirmation via email and SMS once the refund is initiated.

Can I cancel my order after it has been dispatched?

Once an order has been dispatched, you cannot cancel it directly. However, you can refuse to accept the delivery and contact our support team immediately. The order will then be returned to us, and a refund will be processed after deducting applicable delivery charges. Alternatively, you can accept the delivery and initiate a return request within the eligible return window.

What if I receive a defective or wrong product?

If you receive a defective or wrong product, please contact us immediately with your order number and photos of the item. We will arrange for a free pickup or replacement at no additional cost. You are entitled to a full refund or replacement for such cases. Our quality team will investigate the issue to prevent future occurrences.

Will I be charged for returning an item?

Returns are completely free if the issue is from our side (wrong item, defective product, quality issue, etc.). However, if you're returning an item due to a change of mind or personal preference, return shipping charges may apply depending on the product category and order value. Custom orders may have additional charges as specified in their policy.

Can I exchange an item instead of getting a refund?

Yes, we offer exchanges for most products subject to availability. If you'd like to exchange an item for a different size, flavor, or variant, please contact our support team. We'll arrange for the exchange at no additional delivery cost if the issue is from our side. For customer preference exchanges, delivery charges may apply.

What happens if I paid Cash on Delivery?

For Cash on Delivery orders, refunds will be processed via bank transfer (NEFT/IMPS) to your provided bank account details. Please ensure you share your correct bank account information with our support team. The refund will be credited within 5-7 business days after verification. You will need to provide your account number, IFSC code, and account holder name.
