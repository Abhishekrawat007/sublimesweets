Important Note About Delivery Charges

Delivery charges are calculated automatically at checkout based on your location and order value. During festivals and peak seasons, delivery times may be extended by 1-2 days. Special handling charges may apply for bulk orders (20+ kg). Remote area deliveries may incur additional charges which will be communicated before order confirmation.

Special Circumstances

Additional Delivery Information

Understanding special situations and how we handle them.

Public Holidays & Festivals

During major festivals (Diwali, Holi, Eid, Christmas) and public holidays, delivery times may be extended by 24-48 hours due to high demand. We recommend placing orders in advance during festive seasons. Our team works tirelessly to fulfill all orders, but your patience and understanding during peak times is greatly appreciated.

Weather & Natural Events

Extreme weather conditions such as heavy rain, flooding, storms, or other natural events may cause delivery delays. In such cases, we prioritize the safety of our delivery partners and may temporarily suspend deliveries. You will be notified immediately if your delivery is affected, and we'll reschedule at the earliest possible time at no additional cost.

Gift Wrapping & Special Requests

Want to send your order as a gift? We offer complimentary gift wrapping on request! Just add a note during checkout. You can also include personalized messages, greeting cards, and special delivery instructions. For corporate gifting and bulk orders, please contact our dedicated corporate team for customized packaging and delivery solutions.
